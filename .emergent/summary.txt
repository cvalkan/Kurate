**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Ranking Stability as a Stopping Criterion**: Moved from using Wilson Confidence Intervals on individual paper win rates to using Spearman's Rho correlation between rankings over time. This directly measures the stability of the overall leaderboard, which was found to be a much more efficient stopping point.
-   **Pre-computation of Summaries**: Shifted from a lazy, post-hoc generation of impact reports to an eager, pre-computation of three distinct AI summaries at the time of paper ingestion. This decouples summary generation from evaluation and makes summaries instantly available.
-   **Fair-Cost Performance Comparison**: Developed and refined a methodology to compare the efficiency of different evaluation strategies (e.g., summary vs. extract) by normalizing the x-axis of convergence charts to LLM calls per paper, ensuring a true apples-to-apples cost-benefit analysis.
-   **Dynamic Data Pipelines**: Created a reusable pipeline for the Summary Bias experiment that could be parameterized and triggered for different paper categories (, , ).
