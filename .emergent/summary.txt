**Key Technical Concepts**
- **TrueSkill vs Single-Item**: Pairwise (TrueSkill) struggles to beat the Single-Item (SI) baseline when pairs are chosen randomly across completely different topics (cross-label). Intra-topic matchmaking is highly likely to fix this.
- **Equal-weight Pooling**: The Fixed benchmark pools 8 distinct datasets. TrueSkill convergence must be calculated per-dataset and then averaged; otherwise, injecting new datasets into a global pool creates artifacts due to scale-mixing.
- **Match Denominators**: Controlled pairs (humans showed non-tie preference) vs Coin-flip pairs (humans rated both, ties resolved randomly) vs Unique pairs (all matched pairs).
